The number 8 is a Fibonacci number. The Fibonacci numbers – 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, and so on – are found by adding 2 consecutive numbers to get the next one.

As you can see, 8 is the 6th Fibonacci number – hence we sometimes write it as F6.

The number 8 is considered especially auspicious in China because its Cantonese pronunciation, ‘ba’, sounds very simlar to ‘fa’ – meaning good fortune.

Conversely, the number 4 sounds like the word ‘si’ – meaning death. Needless to say, it’s not considered lucky.
